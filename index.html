About Technocraphy BD

Technocraphy BD is a Center for STEAM Education and Research. Focusing on feature and evidence based development of STEAM Education. A student, professional, beginner or a hobbyist can get help from the resources here.

STEAM is an abbreviation for Science, Technology, Engineering, Art and Mathematics. The term is used to describe both education and careers in those fields.

Scholarship News

These scholarships are designed to support primary and high school students pursuing their basic skills and knowledges in STEAM fields. TBD aim's to encourage young minds to explore and excel in science, technology, engineering, art and mathematics disciplines during their elementary studies.
